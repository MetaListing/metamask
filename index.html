What is MetaMask?

MetaMask is a popular browser extension wallet for interacting with the Ethereum blockchain. Developed by ConsenSys in 2016, it now has over 30 million active users and has become an essential tool in the cryptocurrency ecosystem.

At MetaListing, we created this educational guide so that even beginners can learn about MetaMask with confidence. With MetaMask, you can manage ETH and ERC-20 tokens, access DeFi protocols, purchase and store NFTs, and interact with decentralized applications (dApps).

Installation Steps from the Official Site

Below is a guide to the general steps for safely installing MetaMask. Always download from the official site (metamask.io) and be extremely cautious of phishing sites.

1

Access the Official MetaMask Website

Type "metamask.io" directly into your browser's address bar to access the official MetaMask website. We strongly recommend typing the URL directly as links from search engines may lead to fake sites.

2

Click the Download Button on the Official Site

Click the "Download" button on the official site's homepage. Your browser will be automatically detected and you will be redirected to the appropriate official extension store.

3

Verify the Developer and Add from the Official Store

In the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, or your browser's official extension store, make sure to verify that the developer is "MetaMask" before clicking the "Add" button.

4

Confirm Installation is Complete

Once installation is complete, a fox icon will appear in your browser's toolbar. Click this icon to open MetaMask and begin the initial setup following the official instructions.

Beware of Phishing Scams

Many fake websites and extensions impersonate MetaMask. Always download from the official site (metamask.io) and verify in the extension store that the developer is "MetaMask." This site (MetaListing) is not a MetaMask distributor.

Wallet Setup

After installation, when you first open MetaMask, the wallet setup screen will appear. Below is an explanation of the general setup steps. Please refer to the official help center for details.

Creating a New Wallet

1

Select "Create a New Wallet"

Select the "Create a new wallet" option on the initial screen. If you already have a wallet, choose "Import existing wallet."

2

Set a Strong Password

Create a strong password of at least 8 characters. This password will be used to unlock MetaMask in your browser. We recommend combining uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

3

Securely Record Your Secret Recovery Phrase

A 12-word Secret Recovery Phrase will be displayed. This is the only way to recover your wallet. Write it down on paper and store it safely offline. Never save it digitally or share it with anyone.

4

Confirm Your Phrase

Enter your recorded Secret Recovery Phrase in the correct order to confirm. This verifies that you have accurately recorded the phrase.

Security Tip

Consider storing your Secret Recovery Phrase engraved on a fireproof and waterproof metal backup tool. This can protect your phrase from fire and water damage.

Security Tips

To use MetaMask safely, please always practice the following security measures.

Essential Security Measures

Protect Your Secret Recovery Phrase: Store your 12-word phrase offline in multiple secure locations. Never take screenshots, save to cloud storage, or send via email.

Be Vigilant Against Phishing Scams: Official MetaMask will never ask for your Secret Recovery Phrase via email or website. If you receive such a request, it is 100% a scam.

Use Official Channels: MetaMask support is only provided through the official website (metamask.io) and the in-app help center. They do not provide support via DMs on social media.

Regular Security Checks: Regularly check connected sites and disconnect from any sites you no longer use. You can check "Connected sites" in MetaMask settings.

Absolutely Follow This

Anyone who knows your Secret Recovery Phrase can access all the assets in your wallet. No company or support representative, including MetaListing, will ever ask for your Secret Recovery Phrase. If asked, it is 100% a scam.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions and answers about MetaMask.

Yes, downloading and installing the MetaMask extension is completely free. However, when executing transactions on the Ethereum network, network fees (gas fees) will apply. These fees are paid to the Ethereum network, not to MetaMask. For details, please visit the official site metamask.io.

MetaMask supports Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Opera, and Microsoft Edge browsers. Official mobile apps for iOS and Android are also available, allowing you to use it from your smartphone. Always download from the official site or official stores.

The Secret Recovery Phrase (also called a seed phrase) is a 12-word sequence used to recover your wallet. If you lose this, you will not be able to access the assets in your wallet in case of device failure or forgotten password. Always store it safely offline and never share it with anyone.

MetaMask is a safe wallet when downloaded from the official site and used properly. Your private keys are stored locally on your device and are not sent to MetaMask's servers.
